SPATZ

Low-cost Test Rocket

SPATZ is a modular low-cost rocket developed for flights inside Austria.

The project was developed as a bachelor thesis with the goal that the rocket can be adapted to test experimental systems. This helps to make more reliable and safe competiton rockets.

A solid SRAD-Booster is used to reach an altitude of 120m. We use rocket candy and the manufacturing is a good training opportunity for new members of the propulsion module.
Moreover, the rocket can be used to test cubesats which are developed by schools.
