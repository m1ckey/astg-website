AVES

EuRoC 2021 Competition Rocket

Technical Poster

AVES was designed to fly at the European Rocketry Challenge in October 2021 in Portugal.
We used a self-developed solid booster to reach an apogee of 3000â€¯m.

With the Airbrakes, supported by the control-technology of our self-developed Flight-Computer, the rocket reaches the exact target altitude. The top part of the rocket fits three payloads with the form factor of CanSats. The content of the payloads was made in cooperation with students at Higher Technical Institutes. To land save on the ground, a recovery-system with two-stage parachutes was designed.
